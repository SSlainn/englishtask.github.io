One Life Project

Let me introduce myself, my name is Nolan LY and I am a scientist but also one of the founders of the "Kichta Space Program". Our company has currently and definitely 5 co-founders who each manage a different project essential to the functioning of our collaboration. I am going to present you my project which is called "One Life Project" and this project consists in a trip based on the scientific research of distant planets during the whole life of the astronauts who are on board of the spaceship while keeping a permanent means of communication with the Earth to be able to transmit the possible discoveries. The headquarters of the construction of the spacecraft is located on one of the sites of our sponsor, NASA. We will work mainly on the technical and scientific aspects to realize this project because we do not know the possible differences of atmospheric pressure of the different planets which can still be unknown and therefore we will have to find a very resistant material, We will also have to think about different suits to resist to different risks that astronauts can be confronted with and therefore we will have to think about all the possible risks to create these suits in several copies because this "trip of a lifetime" that is the "One Life Project" is a very long term project because as its name indicates it lasts the whole life of the astronauts. To be able to realize this project, it will also be necessary to think of paying attention to the ecology as Elon Musk did it with the reactor of his Falcon 9 rocket which lands on a platform to avoid the pollution of the oceans and thus it will be necessary to look for a way to make return the vessel to its point of takeoff. For ecology, we should also think about materials and fuels that are good for the environment, good for space but also good for a possible extra terrestrial life. For all these ecological researches, we will work particularly with Alexandre NGUYEN, creator of I.K.E.A, a project based on nutrition and logistics, but also with our sponsors. For the economy, we must succeed in creating a quality vessel by keeping a maximum of money aside for the [K.S.P] and for that, we will work with Ethan GUERIN, an economist who will make the money of the [K.S.P] fructified. We have estimated the realization of the project for 2035 but it will be necessary to wait for the end of the production of the 4 other projects which is planned for February 2048.

I thank you for taking the time to read my project and hope you understood and appreciated it. I also hope that you will help us financially to be able to realize this project which will be one of the greatest technical, scientific, economic and ecological innovations in the world.
